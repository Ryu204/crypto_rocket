Crypto Rocket

How to play

  • Spacebar/Press and hold to boost the rocket
  • Over time it will run out of fuel, so collect the shinies to refill your bar
  • You will most likely need to have a crypto wallet to save your high scores (remeber to switch to a Sepolia testnet account)
  • Since the score board is stored on a blockchain you will have to wait a bit until new entry is added
